Frequently Asked Questions

What is WorkshopPro?

WorkshopPro is an automotive workshop management platform for customer intake, jobs, customer records, vehicle history, quotes, invoices, service reminders, and workshop operations.

Who is WorkshopPro designed for?

It is designed for independent mechanics, repair workshops, service centres, and growing automotive businesses.

Can customers scan a QR code when they arrive?

Yes. Workshops can place a QR code on site so customers can scan it on arrival, open the public app, and log either a repair request or a general service request from their phone.

Is the customer app mobile friendly?

Yes. The public experience is built as a mobile-friendly PWA that works well on phones and can be installed for faster repeat bookings.

Is it suitable for workshops in Auckland and New Zealand?

Yes. The platform is built in Auckland and designed for local workshop operations across New Zealand.

Can it manage vehicle service history?

Yes. WorkshopPro stores customer and vehicle history so teams can work with stronger context and continuity.

Can it handle quotes and invoices?

Yes. Staff can create quotes, send them for digital approval, convert approved work into invoices, and generate PDF billing documents.

Is it cloud-based?

Yes. WorkshopPro is a cloud-based SaaS platform with secure web access for staff and a mobile-friendly public PWA for customers.

Can I request a demo?

Yes. Use the contact page to book a demo and discuss your workshop’s setup.
