Executive Summary

Carmaster operates a unified operations and customer engagement platform: a staff portal for workshop management and a public PWA for customer-led job capture. The product combines CRM, jobs, quotes, invoices, reminders, and branded self-service booking into one workflow.

Target users
Workshop owners, service advisors, technicians
Core value
Faster intake, less admin, better follow-up conversion
Differentiator
Staff + customer PWA workflow in one stack

Marketable Product Capabilities

Operations Control

  • Centralized job lifecycle management with search, status handling, and due-date visibility.
  • Customer records tied to vehicle rego, contact data, and historical jobs.
  • Automated service schedule and renewal reminder monitoring.

Revenue Workflow

  • Quote creation and sharing pipeline with digital decision handling.
  • Invoice workflow with PDF generation and outbound communication support.
  • Upsell and package support for maintenance monetization.

Customer Experience

  • Installable public PWA for quick self-service booking and repair requests.
  • Mobile-first intake with WOF/Rego context and guided form progression.
  • Branded experience: Carmaster identity with “powered by Workshop Pro”.

Platform & Security

  • JWT-authenticated staff portal + Office 365 SSO support.
  • Public endpoint throttling and validated request contracts.
  • Configurable settings for business branding and service catalogs.
