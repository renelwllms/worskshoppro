About Us

New Zealand SaaS builders for practical business software.

WorkshopPro is created and hosted by EdgePoint, a New Zealand technology company focused on practical cloud software. The platform is completely hosted in New Zealand and built for local businesses that need reliable SaaS products with strong operational fit.

Who We Are

EdgePoint has supported New Zealand businesses for years with cloud-first technology, responsive service, and a practical approach to solving operational problems.

WorkshopPro reflects that same mindset. We build SaaS applications for the New Zealand market with a focus on usability, workflow clarity, and day-to-day business outcomes rather than generic software bloat.

Built for New Zealand

We work with local businesses and understand the pace, expectations, and operating realities of the New Zealand market.

Long-term mindset

Our goal is not just to ship software, but to build trusted products and relationships that keep delivering value over time.

Cloud-first delivery

We design secure, modern SaaS applications that are easier to access, maintain, and improve as customer needs evolve.

Why WorkshopPro Exists

EdgePoint has worked with businesses across multiple sectors in New Zealand, including automotive operators. That experience showed a clear need for software built around real workshop processes, not generic admin tools.

WorkshopPro was created to give mechanics and workshop teams a more connected way to manage bookings, jobs, customer records, quotes, invoices, and communication.

How We Build

We take a one-team approach: understand the workflow, simplify the moving parts, and deliver software that feels clear from day one.

That means practical onboarding, reliable cloud delivery, and ongoing improvement based on what New Zealand businesses actually need.
