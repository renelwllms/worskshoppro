Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers for workshops researching automotive workshop software, customer QR intake, and mobile-friendly PWA workflows in Auckland and across New Zealand.

What is WorkshopPro?

WorkshopPro is an automotive workshop management platform designed to help mechanics manage customer intake, jobs, customer records, vehicle history, quotes, invoices, service reminders, and day-to-day operations.

Who is WorkshopPro built for?

It is built for automotive mechanics, repair workshops, and service businesses that want a more organised and modern operating system.

Is WorkshopPro suitable for workshops in Auckland?

Yes. WorkshopPro is positioned for workshops in Auckland and across New Zealand, with a focus on practical local business needs.

Can WorkshopPro help reduce admin work?

Yes. The platform is designed to reduce manual admin by organising bookings, job tracking, customer records, and workshop workflow in one place.

Can customers scan a QR code when they arrive?

Yes. Workshops can display a QR code so customers can scan it on arrival, open the public app, and log either a repair request or a general service request from their phone.

Is the customer app mobile friendly?

Yes. The customer experience is a fully mobile-friendly PWA that works well on phones and can be installed for faster repeat bookings.

Does the platform store vehicle history?

Yes. Workshops can maintain vehicle records and service history to support repeat service and clearer job context.

Can WorkshopPro send quotes and invoices?

Yes. Staff can send quotes for digital approval, generate invoice PDFs, and manage billing from the same system.

Can I request a demo?

Yes. Contact us to book a demo and discuss the right setup for your workshop.
