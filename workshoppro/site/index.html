Frequently Asked Questions

What is WorkshopPro?

WorkshopPro is an automotive workshop management platform for bookings, jobs, customer records, vehicle history, quotes, invoices, and workshop operations.

Who is WorkshopPro designed for?

It is designed for independent mechanics, repair workshops, service centres, and growing automotive businesses.

Is it suitable for workshops in Auckland and New Zealand?

Yes. The platform is built in Auckland and designed for local workshop operations across New Zealand.

Can it manage vehicle service history?

Yes. WorkshopPro stores customer and vehicle history so teams can work with stronger context and continuity.

Is it cloud-based?

Yes. WorkshopPro is a cloud-based SaaS platform with secure web access.

Can I request a demo?

Yes. Use the contact page to book a demo and discuss your workshop’s setup.
