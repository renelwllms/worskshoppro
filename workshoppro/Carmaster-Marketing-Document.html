Executive Summary

Carmaster operates a unified operations and customer engagement platform: a staff portal for workshop management and a public PWA for customer-led job capture. The product combines CRM, jobs, quotes, invoices, reminders, and branded self-service booking into one workflow.

A key differentiator is the workshop arrival flow: customers can scan a QR code when they arrive, open the public experience on their phone, then choose whether they want to log a repair job or a general service request. The same customer journey is fully mobile friendly and works as an installable PWA for faster repeat use.

Target users
Workshop owners, service advisors, technicians, and arriving customers
Core value
Faster intake, less admin, better conversion, and stronger repeat service follow-up
Differentiator
Staff portal + QR arrival flow + installable customer PWA in one stack

Customer QR Arrival Flow

  • Step 1: Customer scans the QR code at reception or when arriving at the workshop.
  • Step 2: The public Carmaster experience opens instantly on their phone.
  • Step 3: The customer selects whether they need a repair job or a regular service.
  • Step 4: Vehicle and service details are submitted into the workshop system for staff follow-up.
  • Step 5: The customer can install the PWA for quicker repeat bookings in future.

Why this matters

  • Reduces front-desk bottlenecks during busy arrival periods.
  • Improves data capture quality at the point of intake.
  • Supports a fully mobile-friendly workshop experience.
  • Creates a stronger branded journey from arrival through to follow-up.
