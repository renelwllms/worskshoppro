Simple, powerful workshop software.

WorkshopPro is delivered as a single, complete SaaS platform designed for automotive workshops. Instead of complicated tiers and feature restrictions, every customer gets access to the full system from day one.

The platform includes QR customer check-in, repair and general service intake, job tracking, customer and vehicle records, quotes, invoice PDFs, reminders, technician workflow tools, and operational visibility in one connected system.

Deployment & Onboarding

  • Initial platform setup
  • Workshop workflow configuration
  • User access setup
  • Guidance for bookings, jobs, customer records, and QR intake setup
  • Branding support for the public PWA experience

Support

Workshops receive ongoing support and platform updates as part of the service.
